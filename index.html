Colors on the web are confusing — but they don’t have to be! The HSL format makes it easy for humans and computers to work with color.

HSL: a color format for humans!

The HSL format makes it easy for humans and computers to work with color. When I was learning web development, color codes felt like magic spells: #000000 meant black, #ff0000 meant red, and #ffffff meant white. These are called hexadecimal colors and they’re super confusing.
